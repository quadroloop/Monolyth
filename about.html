Free Web Analytics for Single Page Websites.




Monolyth is a JS Based Web Analytics Platform. It is free and Opensource!, using Akveo Blur Admin Template. Just input your site URL, a password and get your code, then include it to your website. to learn more, click the "How to use" button. Thats it! no Accounts Necessary.
